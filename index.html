Method

How Ordsalat reads a corpus

1. Normalize documents

Every speech becomes a dated record with source, speaker, organization, country, title, URL and cleaned text.

2. Count terms by time bucket

Terms are counted per decade and normalized as mentions per 10,000 words so long and short eras are comparable.

3. Track adoption and velocity

The app records first observed year, total mentions, document count, peak intensity, latest intensity and recent momentum.

4. Preserve source links

Term evidence keeps top speeches and source URLs so interesting spikes can be inspected instead of treated as black-box signals.
